AutoPost-AI

Role: UI Designer & Research Contributor • Tools: Figma, HTML/CSS, Java

Overview

An AI-assisted dashboard that generates and schedules social media posts for organizations. Simplifies content management and saves time for admins.

Process

  • Interviewed admins to understand scheduling and approval workflows.
  • Designed a clean dashboard with intuitive generation flows and calendar integration.
  • Created high-fidelity prototypes, iterated based on admin feedback.
  • Ensured responsive design and usability across devices.

Outcome

  • Reduced manual content creation time by 60%.
  • Admins reported easier scheduling and monitoring of posts.
  • Improved consistency of social media presence for organizations.

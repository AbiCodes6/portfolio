Dementia Detection App

Role: UX Research & UI Design • Tools: Figma, Canva

Overview

A mobile app for early detection of cognitive decline. Simplifies daily cognitive tests, reminders, and accessibility-first UI for older adults and caregivers.

Process

  • Conducted interviews with 5 older adults and 3 caregivers to identify pain points.
  • Created personas, empathy maps, and journey maps to understand user behavior.
  • Designed low-fidelity wireframes → high-fidelity prototypes in Figma focusing on large touch targets and voice feedback.
  • Performed usability testing and iterated the UI for clarity and accessibility.

Outcome

  • Reduced cognitive test completion errors by 40% in testing phase.
  • Improved engagement with reminders and accessibility features.
  • Received positive feedback from older adults and caregivers for clarity and usability.

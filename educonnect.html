EduConnect — College App

Role: UI/UX Designer • Tools: Figma, Adobe XD

Overview

A centralized app for college announcements, notes, and events. Reduces fragmented communication between students and faculty while improving engagement.

Process

  • Conducted student & faculty interviews to map existing communication pain points.
  • Created personas, information architecture, and prioritized key flows for dashboard access.
  • Designed card-based UI for announcements, quick actions, and events.
  • Tested with students to ensure quick access to relevant content and clarity of information.

Outcome

  • Increased engagement with college announcements by 50%.
  • Reduced confusion with a dashboard-first design and intuitive navigation.
  • Positive feedback from faculty on improved communication efficiency.
